GET DOWN TO BUSINESS WITH INDY FRESH

Indy Fresh Catering is a full service catering company offering fresh, local and fabulous food at an affordable price. Indy Fresh was designed to cater any event at any price. We service all of Central Indiana and prepare our fresh, made-to-order food daily in our Indianapolis kitchen. We deliver for free and will set up everything up so you can get down to business!

We understand corporate catering is a reflection on your business. We are seasoned professionals with over 13 years of Indianapolis catering experience. Your food will be correct, on time and delicious! We hope to make your meal the least stressful part of your meeting!
