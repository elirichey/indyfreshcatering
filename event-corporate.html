Indy Fresh is a full service catering company offering fresh, local and fabulous food at a smart price point. With our exclusive Fancy Fresh and Wedding Service, we hope to be the perfect fit for your big day. Fancy Fresh by Indy Fresh Catering is the go-to caterer for any venue, rustic or refined. Our full-service catering house has served over 100,000 happy guests. We are not a restaurant with a catering-side business or a major stuffy catering conglomerate. We credit our humble success to a passion for fabulous food and innovative service and a menu designed to travel perfectly to your site.

We will work with you to create a custom catering package that fits your wedding vision. To please everyone on your guest list, we specialize in Italian, comfort food and casual local barbecue. We can do buffets, tray service, personalized bars and station service. We have bartenders on staff to serve the spirit of your choice. We offer budget friendly plastic ware, eco chic bamboo ware, faux china and even real the real and breakable stuff, upon request.

At Indy Fresh, we know nothing can ruin the mood like a high price tag. We have a very efficient business model, which means we can pass the savings on to you. You can hit the dance floor happy knowing your guests are happy with an incredible meal at a smart price. We pride ourselves in no hidden costs. After all, entertaining is how make your guests feel, not what you spend.

Indy Fresh can cater your parties, rehearsal dinners, luncheons, showers, send-off brunches and snack platters to get you through any celebration in life. The more you use us, the more you will save on your event.

Once we have a good idea of your vision and budget, we will send you a proposal. The proposal will include the food, flatware, linens for the buffet tables and any staff members dedicated to making your event special.
