CATERING PACKAGES

Convenience, for all your corporate and private events

THE WHOLE PACKAGE

Whether it’s for business or pleasure, Indy Fresh Catering offers comprehensive breakfast, lunch and dinner catering packages for all of your corporate and private events. Choosing one of our catering packages is a cost-efficient way to accommodate your group. The minimum order for our affordable packages, which range from $5 to $15 per person, is $150. This setup conveniently includes disposable serving utensils, cutlery, plates, napkins, cups, chafing dishes with warmers and drinks, such as iced tea, lemonade and Coca-Cola. Plus, our menu items are made fresh every day. It’s a no-brainer!

BREAKFAST PACKAGES

From wraps to buffet, start the day off right with one of Indy Fresh’s breakfast packages. Each comes with orange juice, and coffee is available for $1.25 per person.

  • Breakfast Wraps

    Pre-rolled breakfast wraps come with a choice of bacon, Italian sausage or vegetarian fillings, as well as a variety of Danishes, which can be swapped for a fruit platter for an extra $0.50/person.

    9 / person

  • French Toast Buffet

    Custard-dipped, home-baked cinnamon French toast comes with choice of the following toppings: fresh fruit, chocolate chips or homemade rum caramel and a side of bacon or sausage.

    9 / person

  • Pancake Buffet

    Home-baked buttermilk pancakes are served with maple syrup and a choice of toppings: fresh fruit, chocolate chips or homemade rum caramel and a side of bacon or sausage.

    8 / person

  • All American Breakfast

    Traditional scrambled eggs come with house potatoes and bacon or sausage.

    8 / person

DINNER PACKAGES

Set the scene for a great evening affair with Indy Fresh’s home-style Italian, BBQ or Meat and Three packages. Each option comes with an appetizer, drinks and dessert. You also have the option of splitting the order in half with two dinner entrees!

  • Italian Dinner

    This includes dinner portions of our signature side salad, one appetizer, a pasta dish, two of our homemade sauces and two entrees. It also comes with fresh-baked Italian bread, drinks and a dessert option of either homemade bread pudding or brownies.

    15 / person

  • BBQ Dinner

    This includes dinner portions of our house-smoked, slow-roasted pulled pork or chicken sandwiches on slider buns and comes with two sides and JohnTom's BBQ Sauce. Also enjoy Ghirardelli brownies and drinks.

    15 / person

  • Meat & 3

    This includes dinner portions of two of our meat entrees, one appetizer and three of our homemade sides. Plus, enjoy Ghirardelli brownies and drinks.

    15 / person

We have gluten-free, dairy-free, vegan, vegetarian and more options. And please note that all meals will be cooked in the same kitchen facility.
