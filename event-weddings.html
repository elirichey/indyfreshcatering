WEDDINGS

FOR YOUR SPECIAL DAY

Indy Fresh is a full service catering company offering fresh, local and fabulous food at a smart price. We hope to be the perfect fit for your big day. Indy Fresh Catering is the go-to caterer for any venue, rustic or refined. We have a full-service staff of seasoned professionals.

We will work with you to create a custom catering package that fits your wedding vision. To please everyone on your guest list, we we have a variety of menu options. From Italian dishes made with our homemade sauces, pulled pork straight from the smoker, or any of our lighter fares, your guests will keep coming back until their hearts are content! We offer budget friendly plastic ware, eco chic bamboo ware, faux china, and even the real breakable stuff, upon request.

Once we have a good idea of your vision and budget, we will send you a proposal that includes the food, flatware, linens for the buffet tables and any staff members dedicated to making your event special. We deliver for free within the I-465 perimeter and we deliver outside of the I-465 area for a nominal charge. We will set up everything so you can breath easy and enjoy the party!
