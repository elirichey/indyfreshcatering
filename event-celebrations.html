CELEBRATE WITH INDY FRESH

Indy Fresh Catering is a full service catering company offering fresh, local and fabulous food at an affordable price. Indy Fresh was designed to cater any event at any price. We service all of Central Indiana and prepare our fresh, made-to-order food daily in our Indianapolis kitchen. We deliver for free within a 20 mile radius of our location ($20 within 40 miles & $40 witin 60 miles) and will set up everything up so you can breath easy and enjoy the party!
