SANDWICHES & WRAPS

Sandwiches and wraps are available in fresh baked grinder bread, Italian bread, spinach wrap, tomato wrap, or garden wrap.

  • Classic Italian

    Midwestern brown sugar ham, salami and pepperoni with mixed greens, vine ripe tomato, yellow onion, lemon herb dressing on fresh baked Italian grinder bread.

    6" Sub or Full Wrap: 6

  • Authentic Italian

    Thinly sliced Maestro Italian Prosciutto di Parma with aged sliced provolone, mixed baby greens, virgin olive oil and herb seasoning, served on fresh baked Italian bread.

    6" Sub or Full Wrap: 8

  • Turkey Avocado

    Oven roasted turkey, vine ripe tomatoes, red onion, fresh mozzarella, mixed greens, roasted garlic aioli.

    6" Sub or Full Wrap: 6

  • Turkey Club

    Oven roasted turkey, applewood bacon, mixed greens, vine ripe tomatoes, roasted garlic aioli.

    6" Sub or Full Wrap: 6

  • Turkey & Ham

    Oven roasted turkey, brown sugar ham, mixed greens, provolone, lemon herb dressing.

    6" Sub or Full Wrap: 6

  • Chicken Chipotle

    Marinated roasted chicken breast, baby greens, chipotle aioli, red onion, parmesan cheese.

    6" Sub or Full Wrap: 7

  • Chicken Bacon Ranch

    Marinated roasted chicken breast, baby greens, applewood bacon, cheddar and ranch.

    6" Sub or Full Wrap: 7

  • Chicken Caesar

    Marinated roasted chicken breast, crisp romaine hearts, classic Caesar dressing, parmesan.

    6" Sub or Full Wrap: 7

  • Veggie & Herb

    Grilled yellow squash, zucchini, red peppers and portobello mushrooms, fresh mozzarella, mixed baby greens, balsamic dressing.

    6" Sub or Full Wrap: 7
