SMART

Indy Fresh does one thing and we do it well. Catering. We make incredible food with affordable prices. We are a commercial kitchen that doesn't incur the expense of running a full service restaurant and that is how we pass the value onto you. Indy Fresh splurges where it counts. We use only high quality, fresh ingredients that we create in-house every day. Indy Fresh Catering offers bundled menu items to cater to any event at any price point. What a fresh idea!

LOCAL

We are from Indy, we live in Indy and we are just as passionate about our food as we are our hometown. Indy Fresh is a local full-service catering company that prepares fresh meals with fresh ingredients every day. Indy Fresh Catering delivers to all of Central Indiana. We are proud to be your hometown, local catering company delivering fresh made to order food every day.
